MY FAVOURITE MUSIC ALBUM PLAYLIST!

As an ardent music lover, I have never listened to an album I disliked. Music to me is all about perspective. Its about understanding the reason behind the lyrics, the story behind the symphony and the spirit in the sound.

This playlist consists of songs and albums that spoke to me at a point in my life when I felt like no one else was listening. I hope that it can be the light at the end of your tunnel as well. have a good listen!
